## Red Flag Probes

One observation per probe. Where a single upstream flag splits into multiple probes (e.g. `postpartum_haemorrhage` → `_volume` / `_clots` / `_haemodynamic`), all children are listed under the same header with their `parent_flag_code`.

### `infant_feeding_failure` (split into 2 probes)

**`infant_feeding_intake`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> Is the baby having at least six wet nappies a day?

_NICE basis:_ NG194 §1.4

**`infant_feeding_weight_concern`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> Has the midwife raised any concerns about the baby's weight — weight loss or slow weight gain?

_NICE basis:_ NG194 §1.4

### `pe_symptoms` (split into 2 probes)

**`pe_symptoms_breathing`** → acute_shortness_of_breath / 999
> Have you had any sudden breathlessness that wasn't there before?

_NICE basis:_ NG89 §1.3 / NG158

**`pe_symptoms_chest_pain`** → chest_pain / 999
> Any sharp chest pain — especially when you breathe in deeply?

_NICE basis:_ NG89 §1.3 / NG158

### `postnatal_depression_severe` (split into 2 probes)

**`postnatal_depression_active_ideation`** → suicidal_ideation / 999
> Have you had any thoughts about harming yourself, or about ending your own life?

_NICE basis:_ NG192 §1.6 / CG192 §1.9

**`postnatal_depression_passive_ideation`** → suicidal_ideation / same_day
> Have you had any thoughts that you or your family would be better off without you?

_NICE basis:_ NG192 §1.6 / CG192 §1.9

### `postpartum_haemorrhage` (split into 3 probes)

**`postpartum_haemorrhage_clots`** → haemorrhage / 999
> Have you passed any clots that were bigger than a 50p coin?

_NICE basis:_ NG192 §1.5.8

**`postpartum_haemorrhage_haemodynamic`** → haemorrhage / 999
> Have you felt faint, dizzy, or lightheaded — especially when you stand up?

_NICE basis:_ NG192 §1.5.8

**`postpartum_haemorrhage_volume`** → haemorrhage / 999
> Have you been soaking through a maternity pad in less than an hour?

_NICE basis:_ NG192 §1.5.8

### `pre_eclampsia_signs` (split into 3 probes)

**`pre_eclampsia_epigastric`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> Any pain in the upper right part of your tummy, just below the ribs?

_NICE basis:_ NG133 §1.5 / NG192 §1.5.6

**`pre_eclampsia_headache`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> Have you had a severe headache that doesn't go away with simple painkillers?

_NICE basis:_ NG133 §1.5 / NG192 §1.5.6

**`pre_eclampsia_visual`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> Any changes to your vision — blurring, flashing lights, or spots?

_NICE basis:_ NG133 §1.5 / NG192 §1.5.6

### `wound_dehiscence` (split into 2 probes)

**`wound_dehiscence_discharge`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> Is there any pus or bloody fluid coming from the wound?

_NICE basis:_ NG192 §1.5.3

**`wound_dehiscence_gaping`** → pathway_specific / 999
> Has the wound opened up at all — the edges of the scar coming apart?

_NICE basis:_ NG192 §1.5.3
