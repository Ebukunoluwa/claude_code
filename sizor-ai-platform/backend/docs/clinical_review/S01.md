## Red Flag Probes

One observation per probe. Where a single upstream flag splits into multiple probes (e.g. `postpartum_haemorrhage` → `_volume` / `_clots` / `_haemodynamic`), all children are listed under the same header with their `parent_flag_code`.

### `anticoagulant_non_adherence` (1 probe)

**`anticoagulant_non_adherence`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> Have you missed any doses of the blood-thinning tablets in the last few days, or run out of your supply?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.5

### `bp_severely_uncontrolled` (1 probe)

**`bp_severely_uncontrolled`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> If you've been checking your blood pressure at home — has the top number been over 180 on any reading in the last 24 hours?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.6

### `dysphagia_aspiration` (split into 3 probes)

**`dysphagia_chest_infection_signs`** → sepsis_signs / 999
> Have you had a new chesty cough, fever, or feeling hot-and-cold in the last 24 hours — especially if you've been coughing when you eat or drink?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.7 / NG51

**`dysphagia_coughing_choking`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> Have you been coughing or choking on food or drink in the last 24 hours, or felt that food has gone down the wrong way?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.7

**`dysphagia_wet_voice`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> After eating or drinking, has your voice sounded wet, gurgly, or like you need to clear your throat to speak clearly?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.7

### `falls_with_injury` (split into 3 probes)

**`falls_with_injury_head_strike`** → pathway_specific / 999
> If you've had a fall, did you hit your head, black out, or can't remember the fall itself?

_NICE basis:_ QS2 / NG128

**`falls_with_injury_new_pain`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> If you've had a fall, has it left you with any new pain — in your arms, shoulders, back, hips, or legs?

_NICE basis:_ QS2 / NG128

**`falls_with_injury_new_weakness`** → new_focal_neuro / 999
> If you've had a fall, has anyone noticed you seem weaker on one side, or having more trouble moving or speaking, since the fall?

_NICE basis:_ QS2 / NG128

### `haemorrhage` (split into 4 probes)

**`haemorrhage_blood_in_urine_or_stool`** → haemorrhage / same_day
> Any blood when you pee, or dark or bright blood in your poo, in the last 24 hours?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.5

**`haemorrhage_major_bleed`** → haemorrhage / 999
> Have you vomited blood, coughed up blood, or had bleeding that you couldn't stop — from any part of your body — today?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.5

**`haemorrhage_prolonged_nosebleed`** → haemorrhage / same_day
> Have you had any nosebleeds that didn't stop after 10 minutes of pinching the nose, in the last 24 hours?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.5

**`haemorrhage_unusual_bruising`** → haemorrhage / same_day
> Any large bruises that have appeared without a bump or knock, or bruises that are bigger than the palm of your hand?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.5

### `new_neurological_symptoms` (split into 2 probes)

**`new_neurological_seizure`** → new_focal_neuro / 999
> Has anyone around you witnessed a seizure or fit — shaking movements, loss of awareness, or an odd episode you can't remember afterwards — in the last few days?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.4

**`new_neurological_severe_headache`** → new_focal_neuro / 999
> Have you had any severe headache that came on very suddenly — like a thunderclap, or the worst headache you can remember — in the last 24 hours?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.4

### `stroke_recurrence_signs` (split into 3 probes)

**`stroke_recurrence_arm_weakness`** → new_focal_neuro / 999
> Has anyone around you noticed your arm looking weaker on one side today — dropping things, struggling to lift a kettle, or not moving the same as the other arm?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.3

**`stroke_recurrence_face_drooping`** → new_focal_neuro / 999
> Has anyone noticed one side of your face looking droopy or uneven today — like one corner of your mouth not moving the same as the other?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.3

**`stroke_recurrence_speech_difficulty`** → new_focal_neuro / 999
> Has your speech been slurred or harder to get out today, or has anyone said they're having trouble understanding you?

_NICE basis:_ NG128 §1.3
