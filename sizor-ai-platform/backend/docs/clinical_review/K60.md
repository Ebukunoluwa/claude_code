### Day 1-3

| Domain | Question |
|---|---|

## Red Flag Probes

One observation per probe. Where a single upstream flag splits into multiple probes (e.g. `postpartum_haemorrhage` → `_volume` / `_clots` / `_haemodynamic`), all children are listed under the same header with their `parent_flag_code`.

### `acute_confusion` (1 probe)

**`acute_confusion_new`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> Has anyone around you noticed you've become more muddled, confused, or less sharp than usual in the last 24 hours?

_NICE basis:_ NG106 §1.6

### `anuric_or_oliguria` (1 probe)

**`anuric_or_oliguria`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> Have you not peed at all for more than 12 hours, or only passed a small amount once or twice all day — despite drinking fluids?

_NICE basis:_ NG106 §1.6

### `bp_crisis_above_180` (1 probe)

**`bp_crisis_above_180`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> If you've been checking your blood pressure at home — has the top number been over 180 on any reading in the last 24 hours?

_NICE basis:_ CG187 §1.3 / NG136

### `breathlessness_at_rest` (split into 2 probes)

**`breathlessness_at_rest`** → acute_shortness_of_breath / 999
> Have you been breathless at rest — needing to work hard to breathe even while sitting still?

_NICE basis:_ NG106 §1.4 / CG187

**`breathlessness_orthopnoea_new`** → acute_shortness_of_breath / 999
> Have you woken up suddenly gasping for air in the last 24 hours, or had to sit bolt upright in bed to catch your breath?

_NICE basis:_ NG106 §1.4 / CG187

### `chest_pain_at_rest` (1 probe)

**`chest_pain_at_rest`** → chest_pain / 999
> Have you had chest pain that came on at rest — while sitting still, lying down, or not doing anything?

_NICE basis:_ NG106 / NG185

### `chest_pain_on_minimal_exertion` (1 probe)

**`chest_pain_on_minimal_exertion`** → chest_pain / same_day
> Have you had chest pain come on with a small effort today — like standing up from a chair or walking across a room?

_NICE basis:_ NG106 / NG185

### `oxygen_saturation_below_92` (1 probe)

**`oxygen_saturation_below_92`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> If you have a pulse oximeter at home — that's the little finger clip that reads your blood oxygen — has the reading been below 92?

_NICE basis:_ NG106 §1.4

### `uncontrolled_oedema` (split into 2 probes)

**`oedema_new_site`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> Is there any new swelling in your tummy or around your middle, or is your face or hands looking puffy in the last 24 hours?

_NICE basis:_ NG106 §1.4

**`oedema_sudden_worsening`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> Has the swelling in your legs or ankles suddenly got much worse in the last 24 hours, even though you've been taking the water tablets?

_NICE basis:_ NG106 §1.4

### `weight_gain_2kg_2days` (1 probe)

**`weight_gain_2kg_2days`** → pathway_specific / same_day
> Have you gained more than about 4 pounds — or 2 kilos — in the last two days on your morning weighing?

_NICE basis:_ NG106 §1.4
